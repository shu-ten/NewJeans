“Hello, we are NewJeans!”

Also known as 뉴진스, NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, consisting of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin and Hyein. They debuted on August 1, 2022 with their first EP, New Jeans.

Concept

Their concept focuses on authenticity and youthful charm. With music and visuals inspired by early 2000s culture, they connect with fans through relatable and fun storytelling.

Fandom

Their fandom is called Bunnies (Tokki/토끼).

Fun Facts

  • Their name is a play on “new genes,” symbolizing a new generation of pop music.
  • On July 1, teaser videos were released online, announcing their debut track “Attention.”
